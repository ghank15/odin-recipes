NJ Breakfast Stromboli

Description

A classic New Jersey breakfast sandwich redefined, this new take on a classic pizzeria staple is the ultimate crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza Dough, rested at room temperature(from a pizza restaraunt, not store bought)
  • 2 packages Taylor Pork Roll, 8 count(thin sliced)
  • 1 Lb Land o' Lakes American Cheese, sliced fresh from the deli.
  • 6 Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • (Optional)Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook the Taylor Ham; set aside.
  2. Fry eggs until over hard; set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 375
  4. Flour a large surface
  5. Using a rolling pin, roll out dough in a rectangular shape until thin as possible
  6. Layer Taylor Ham (1 dough usually holds ~12 pieces
  7. Layer eggs (1 dough usually holds ~6 eggs)
  8. Layer cheese (~appx 6-8 slices)
  9. Roll it up
  10. Place on greased baking sheet
  11. Brush with olive oil
  12. (Optional)Sprinkle Everything Bagel Seasoning on top of stromboli
  13. Bake for ~30 minutes, until brown on top
  14. Enjoy!
