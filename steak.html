Steak

Description

Steak, grilled or seared to perfection, is one of the simplest and delicious dishes you can make!

Ingredients

  • 1 Steak (Ribeye or NY Strip preferred)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season all sides of your steak liberally with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a well-seasoned cast-iron skillet or grill until scorching hot.
  3. Place steak on cooking surface.
  4. Flip once per minute until center reaches desired cooking temperature. (125/medium-rare, 135/medium)
  5. Allow steak rest 5 minutes before cutting.
  6. Enjoy!
