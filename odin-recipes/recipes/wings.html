Chipotle Wings

Description

These oven-baked wings are so crispy you won't know they weren't fried!

Ingredients

  • 12 Chicken Wings
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chili Powder
  • Chipotle Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. In a bowl, combine seasonings, 1 Tbsp each, except 1/2 Tbsp black pepper and 1/2 Tbsp cumin; mix
  3. Dry chicken wings with a paper towel
  4. Season wings
  5. Place on greased oven pan top-side up
  6. Bake for 30 minutes
  7. Flip wings, bake for 15 minutes
  8. Flip wings one last time, bake for 10-15 minutes or until crispy
  9. Let cool, enjoy!
